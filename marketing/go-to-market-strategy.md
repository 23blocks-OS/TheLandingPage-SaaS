## Risk Mitigation

### Low Engagement on Launch
**Mitigation**:
- Pre-build email list of 100+
- Line up 10 friends for initial upvotes
- Schedule across time zones
- Have backup content ready

### Negative Feedback
**Mitigation**:
- Respond professionally and quickly
- Address technical concerns transparently
- Create issues for valid bugs
- Show roadmap for requested features

### Technical Issues
**Mitigation**:
- Extensive pre-launch testing
- Have team on standby
- Clear status page
- Quick rollback plan

### Slow Adoption
**Mitigation**:
- Identify barriers through surveys
- Offer white-glove onboarding
- Create more tutorials
- Partner with early users
